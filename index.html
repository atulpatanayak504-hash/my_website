Our Story

Founded in 2019, Foodies has been dedicated to bringing you the best in culinary delights. Our mission is to inspire and empower food lovers around the world with delicious recipes, cooking tips, and food culture insights.
Our founder Chef Walter Patro Habibi, is a visionary leader who believes in the power of food to bring people together. He developed his culinary skills from his childhood. At first, it was responsibility but slowly it grew to passion. Learning from his mother and grandmother, he mastered the art of cooking traditional dishes with a modern twist. He created Foodies to share the food he enjoyed from his childhood with the world. He started his journey by selling street food and then gradually expanded to a full-fledged restaurant. Today, Foodies is a thriving community of food enthusiasts who share a love for good food and great company.

Our Team

Our team of passionate food enthusiasts, chefs, and writers work tirelessly to create content that is both informative and engaging. We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a way to connect with others and celebrate life. They were an instant fan of Chef Walter's cooking and joined him in his mission to share the joy of food with the world. Our team is committed to providing you with the best possible experience on our website, and we welcome your feedback and suggestions. They want to make Foodies a platform where food lovers can come together, share their experiences, and learn from each other.
